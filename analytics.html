Analyze and Export Instagram Data

Want to study your Stories or Highlights performance? Use our analytics page to export data from public profiles into convenient CSV or spreadsheet files. Gain insights into your engagement and keep a personal archive of your favorite content.

Back to Home

Features

  • Export highlights: Download lists of story highlights with dates, captions and media links to CSV/Excel.
  • Content archive: Keep a personal archive of your favourite stories and reels for future reference.
  • Easy analysis: Open exported data in Excel or Google Sheets to track engagement and plan your marketing.
  • No software needed: Everything works in the browser; no sign‑in or installation required.
  • Privacy friendly: We do not store any of your data — you control what you export.

How to Use

  1. Open Instagram and navigate to the story highlight or profile whose data you want to export.
  2. Copy the link and paste it into our export tool on the home page.
  3. Select the export format (CSV or Excel) and click the export button.
  4. Open the downloaded file in your favourite spreadsheet application to analyze the data.
